Application process

Eight steps. We tell you who is responsible at each stage.

Phase 1 — Philippines

Register and prepare

  1. Step 1 [Fuji-Mayon] Free online registration.
  2. Step 2 [Fuji-Mayon] Document check and interview.
  3. Step 3 [Fuji-Mayon] Online orientation (system, conditions, procedure).
Phase 2 — Job & visa

Interview and visa

  1. Step 4 [Fuji-Mayon + Employer] Online interview (Zoom) with a care facility.
  2. Step 5 [Employer + Association] Employment contract explained.
  3. Step 6 [Association] Visa application support.
Phase 3 — Japan

Arrival and work start

  1. Step 7 [Association + Employer] Airport pick-up if needed, housing, city hall procedures.
  2. Step 8 [Employer] Move-in and workplace orientation. You start as a caregiver.

How to avoid illegal recruiters

Work with a legal, transparent process. Here’s what to look for.

Red flags (avoid)

  • Anyone asking for placement fee or “processing fee” before you get a job
  • Vague or unclear contract terms
  • No written explanation of who does what (recruiter vs. employer vs. support org)
  • Pressure to pay or sign quickly
